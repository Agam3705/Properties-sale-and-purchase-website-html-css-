Welcome to the help center of GLOBAL PROPERTY-Real Estate Property Dealings, the best place to find your dream home. We are here to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have regarding our services.

Here are some of the frequently asked questions and their answers:

  • How can I search for properties on your website?

    • You can use the search bar on the homepage to enter your preferred location, budget, property type, and other filters. You can also browse through our featured listings and categories to find the best deals.

  • How can I contact the seller or agent of a property?

    • Once you find a property that interests you, you can click on the "Contact Seller" or "Contact Agent" button on the property page. You will be asked to fill out a form with your name, email, phone number, and message. The seller or agent will get back to you as soon as possible.

  • How can I list my property on your website?

    • If you want to sell or rent your property through our website, you can register as a seller or agent and create your profile. Then you can click on the "Add Property" button and fill out the details of your property. You can also upload photos and videos of your property to attract more buyers or renters.
